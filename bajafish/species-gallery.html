Vermilion Rockfish

Sebastes miniatus — "Red Rockcod"

The iconic red-orange rockfish of Pacific Baja — brilliant vermilion flanks, long-lived (up to 60 years), and one of the most prized table fish along the Pacific coast. Found over rocky structure from 100–1,200 ft.
